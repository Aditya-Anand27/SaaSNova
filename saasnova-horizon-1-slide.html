Horizon 1: Human-Led, AI-Assisted

Reduce manual work and improve visibility without changing SaaSNova's business model.

Horizon 1 principle Capture today's GTM work so tomorrow's AI can scale it.
Goal

Make Jen's operating work visible.

Horizon 1 gives the team a shared view of customer status, readiness, blockers, next actions, and ownership while keeping humans in control.

Why this matters

It attacks the current bottleneck.

Manual GTM execution tasks are valuable but time-consuming. SaaSNova can scale more confidently when repeatable work is captured, tracked, and assisted by AI.

Jen described the current phase as human-led: hiring operators and partner managers while documenting workflows and turning those workflows into training data for the agent.
