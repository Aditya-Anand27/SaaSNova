Our Story How SaaSNova came to be

SaaSNova was built for one reason: GTM teams at ISVs are drowning in manual work that shouldn't be manual.

From chasing co-sell opportunities in spreadsheets to copy-pasting marketplace listings and guessing at attribution — the operational overhead is enormous, and it compounds with every new partner, channel, and campaign.

We believe the next wave of GTM isn't just AI-assisted — it's agentic. A coordinated team of agents that handles the operational layer end-to-end, so your human team can focus on relationships, strategy, and growth.
